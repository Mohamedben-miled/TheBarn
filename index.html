❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about The Barn coworking space

What's included in the monthly membership?

+

Your monthly membership includes unlimited access to our workspace, high-speed 5G WiFi, free coffee, meeting room credits, and access to our game zone with chess boards. Premium members also get a reserved desk with screen and full access until 1:00 AM daily.

What are your operating hours?

+

We're open Monday to Saturday from 8:30 AM to 1:00 AM. We're closed on Sundays. All members have access during our full operating hours.

Do you offer student discounts?

+

Yes! We offer special student pricing with flexible options: 120 DT per month, 8 DT per half day, or 12 DT per full day. Just bring your valid student ID to qualify for these discounted rates. Perfect for students who need a quiet, professional space to study and work.

Can I book meeting rooms?

+

Absolutely! All monthly members get meeting room credits included. Premium members have unlimited access to meeting rooms. Each room is equipped with projectors, whiteboards, and high-speed internet. Book in advance through our reception or call us at 54451627.

Is parking available?

+

Yes, we provide free parking for all our members. The parking area is secure and conveniently located right next to our building on Rue de l'or in Soukra, Ariana.
